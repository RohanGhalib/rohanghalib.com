About Me


 Computers!
I turn logic into software. Primarily use MERN Stack for coding. I treat programming as a craft. This ranges from debugging PHP to tweaking pixels in Illustrator. Recently, I’ve been bridging the gap to the physical world with Raspberry Pi mechatronics and even built my own Linux distro, GhalibOS. When not shipping code, I organize Hack Club events in Bahawalpur.

 Islami Jamiat Talaba (IJT)
My ideological anchor. I am a member of Islami Jamiat Talaba, dedicating my life, prayer, and sacrifice to the principles of Allah. I channel my technical skills to modernize this mission. I move beyond logistics to articulate a narrative of discipline, unity, and spiritual purpose for the digital age.
